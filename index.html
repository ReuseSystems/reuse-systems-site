About RE-USE.Systems

At RE-USE.Systems, we are dedicated to preserving RAF history through the careful curation and sale of authentic MOD aviation equipment. Our mission is to keep the legacy of the Royal Air Force alive by providing enthusiasts and professionals with genuine RAF collectibles and vintage aircraft parts.

We specialize in UK MOD surplus, including aircraft components, aviation test equipment, military communications systems, and historical RAF memorabilia. Each piece in our inventory helps tell the story of British aviation heritage while promoting sustainability through reuse.
